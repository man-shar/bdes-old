Trucker, an app for truck drivers to manage their trips

Background: This project was carried out in the late 2015, during the winter vacations. This app is designed mainly for truck drivers and their customers for better management of the pre-trip works, that is, the finding a person for the requirement, be it on both sides of business. The app helps truck drivers to find return trips, additional trips, manage trips through a calendar and manage the finances involved in the transportation.

Project Mentor: Self Guided Project

   

Duration: December 2015 - January 2016
