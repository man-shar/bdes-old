<Project Name>
SUPPLY CHAIN MANAGEMENT IN THE SILK INDUSTRY OF ASSAM



<brief>
This project is about improving the supply chain management in the silk industry of Assam to 
ensure that handloom weavers get fair prices for their produce. To achieve this, the study proposes to
provide better marketing opportunities and access to customers for weavers corruption of middlemen.



<duration>
Jan-April 2016

<mentor>

Asst. Prof. Pratul Kalita, IIT Guwahati

<Project Name>
Design Reserach and intervention in unorganised sector of India

<brief>
This project is about reducing occupational hazards found in the field of unorganized sector in India. For this project
